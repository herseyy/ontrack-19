Steps

A step-by-step procedure on how to use our website.

  • Step 1: Send Code

    Once a person is tested as positive, she/he is required to give his/her data for tracking. The covid patients must first open and go to this website(link nung website).

  • Step 2: Wait for lgu's call

    Then, the patients will input his/her information in the website- Birthday, Date tested as COVID positve, Sex, Barangay, Contact Number, Asymptomatic or Symptomatic, and status. Every information is required to fill-up. Patient's Id will be automatically assigned.
    As this information except to your contact number will be displayed on the ONTRAC-19 website, the barangay captain will also be informed via text messages saying that his/her member of the barangay is tested positive.

  • Step 3: Give information

    The patients will be quarantined for 10 days as stated in Safety Health Protocol. After 10 days, the patient will receive a link concerning your health status via text messages.

  • Step 4: After ten days, wait for lgu's call again

    The patient will open the link and update his/her information to the website. The link will contain the same information as the form filled-up before the quarantine start. If the patient is unable to respond, the barangay captain will be the one who will go to the website and update the patient's health status.
