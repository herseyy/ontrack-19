ONTRAC-19

ONTRAC-19 is a website that provides data and information to the Tayabas community about COVID-19's status in the city.

To better inform the public, this is a website that makes the information more accessible and easier to find. Its goal is to give information about the status of COVID-19.

Steps

A step-by-step procedure on how to use our website.

  • Step 1: Send Text and Wait

    Once a person is tested positive, they are required to contact us at the information above. They must follow the replied instructions given by the LGU team. Then, wait for the LGU's call.

  • Step 2: Give information

    The patients will give their informations to the LGU and the LGU will make sure that the patient's personal information is exclusive and confidential. The patients will then be quarantined for 10 days as stated in the Safety Health Protocol.

  • Step 3: After ten days, expect a call from the LGU

    After 10 days, the LGU will contact the patient regarding an update about their health status.
